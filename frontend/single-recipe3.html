Bread and egg delight

Make your poached eggs as usual, up to five days before you plan to serve them. Immediately transfer the poached eggs to a bowl of very cold water. Top off with more cold water if needed until the eggs are cool. Transfer the eggs to an airtight container and refrigerate until needed. To reheat, transfer your eggs to a bowl of very hot tap water for 2 to 3 minutes. (Don't use boiling water or your eggs might overcook). Top off the bowl with more hot water if needed. To serve, gently scoop the eggs out of the water with a slotted spoon, blot them dry on a paper towel, and serve immediately.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

2 servings

ingredients

2 slice bread

2 eggs

1 avocado
