Overnight Oats

Overnight oats are a useful option for you who like to eat a quick, tasty and nutritious breakfast in the morning. Plus, it's so easy to make! With my basic recipe and different seasonings / toppings, you can vary overnight oats so that it always feels new and exciting. Quick summary:

1 recipe + 15 flavors = endless breakfast options.

cook time

5 min.
